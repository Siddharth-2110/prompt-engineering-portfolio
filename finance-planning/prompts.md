# Finance Planning Prompt

## 🎯 Objective

Use AI to simulate long-term investment growth and withdrawal strategy (SIP + SWP) without depleting capital.

---

## 🔹 Prompt: SIP Growth + SWP Strategy

**Prompt:**
Act as a financial advisor. I invest ₹15,000 per month as SIP across 5 mutual funds. Calculate the estimated corpus after 15 years assuming a reasonable annual return. Also suggest a Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) strategy so that I can withdraw monthly income without depleting the principal amount.

---

## 🔹 Output (Sample)

**Investment Assumptions:**

* Monthly SIP: ₹15,000
* Duration: 15 years
* Expected Return: ~12% annually

**Estimated Corpus:**
₹75–80 lakhs (approx.)

**SWP Strategy:**

* Withdraw 6–7% annually (~₹35,000–₹40,000/month)
* Keep withdrawal rate lower than expected returns
* Ensure funds remain invested for continued growth

---

## 🔹 Techniques Used

* Role-based prompting (financial advisor)
* Scenario simulation
* Constraint-based output (time + return assumption)
* Multi-part query handling

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

* AI can assist in financial planning scenarios
* Assumptions drive output accuracy
* Structuring prompts improves clarity in multi-step problems
