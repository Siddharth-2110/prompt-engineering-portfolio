# Caption Generator Prompts

## 🎯 Objective

Generate engaging, witty, and trend-based social media captions with tone control.

---

## 🔹 Prompt 1: Trend-Based Caption

**Prompt:**
Generate a witty Instagram caption comparing “2012 vs now” with a confident and slightly savage tone. Keep it short and catchy.

**Output:**
2012 (Class 6): Stress was homework.
Now: Stress has a LinkedIn profile.

**Techniques Used:**

* Tone injection (witty/savage)
* Brevity constraint
* Context comparison

---

## 🔹 Prompt 2: Music/Performance Caption

**Prompt:**
Write a catchy caption for a keyboard performance of “Rasputin” following a trending reel format. Include energy and vibe.

**Output:**
Following the trend, but with my own keys 🎹🔥
Rasputin hits different when you feel every note.

**Techniques Used:**

* Trend alignment
* Emotion layering
* Personal branding tone

---

## 🔹 Prompt 3: Festival Caption

**Prompt:**
Write a caption for a Poila Boishakh lunch with colleagues. Keep it warm, cultural, and cheerful.

**Output:**
Poila Boishakh vibes, good food, and even better company ❤️✨
Starting the Bengali New Year on a perfect note.

**Techniques Used:**

* Cultural context injection
* Positive tone control
* Audience relatability

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings

* Tone dramatically impacts engagement
* Short captions perform better for social media
* Context (festival, trend, mood) improves relevance
